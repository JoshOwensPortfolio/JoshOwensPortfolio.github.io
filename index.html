Projects

Cubus

Cubus is a procedurally generated 2D physics-based platformer where the objective is to explore a procedurally generated cave until you reach your goal, all while avoiding enemies that spawn throughout the cave. I've been working on Cubus for two years now, and I'm expecting to publish it on Android and iOS by February of 2019.

Radar

Radar was a proof-of-concept build I made for a larger game that's in the beginning stages of development. I made Radar to showcase the game's central mechanic - that the player is blind to the world and has to navigate using radar.
